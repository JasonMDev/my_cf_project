Frequently Asked Questions

Have a look here for more detailed information.

Why did you decide to become a web developer?

I love creating things as well as constantly improving my craft. I love learning and being a web developer you have to constanly learn as the technology evolves.

What is your design process?

I stick to a methodology of "Separation of Concerns". With this I mean I focus on the what the client wants. Then I build the basic structure. Later I look at the stylingof the pages. Then I would add interactive features. And repeat. Repeat. REPEAT.

Who are your clients?

My clients are non-profit and close friends with a bit of free lancing on the side. Later as I build my reputation I would like to work on bigger projects.

What is your average project turnaround?

For basic Web Pages: 1 month For Web Applications: 2 - 3 months.
